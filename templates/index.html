About ResponseGen

ResponseGen is a conversational AI powered by the Gemini API. It can answer questions, provide information, and assist with various tasks.

How to Use

  1. Type your question or prompt in the input box below.
  2. Press "Enter" or click the button to submit.
  3. Wait for ResponseGen to generate a response.
  4. Scroll through the chat to view responses.

Start by trying out some sample questions below!

Sample Questions

  • Tell me a joke.
  • Explain how photosynthesis works.
  • What's the capital of France?
